WHY BARNBRIDGE

Fixed Means Fixed

BarnBridge's fixed rates allow you to easily tackle four key pillars: diversification, capital preservation, income, and protection from inflation. The yield earned during lorem*100 the prior epoch for the pool guarantees the current epoch's return.

BarnBridge DAO Levels Up Your Fixed Yield Position.

The LP position is taken by the BarnBridge DAO to power up the fixed yield side of the pool. It only earns a nominal funding rate on deposits. All of the yield that is accrued by LP and fixed yield deposits until maturity is realized yield for the next bond period.

Borrow Against Your Fixed Rate Position.

When you deposit into the pool, you get proof of liquidity tokens that represent your share of the pool. You can therefore borrow against these tokens at originator markets to lever up your returns.

Grow Treasury Value And Hedge Against Fiat Inflation.

BarnBridge is an ideal solution for funds, DAO treasuries, companies, and family offices to earn fixed rate yield on treasury assets while retaining the ability to exit if the need arises.
